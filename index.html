HOME
Fantasy Swiftball is an online game where you try to guess outfits that Taylor Swift wears every night of her Eras Tour, with surprise songs and other stuff like how long the cheer after 'champagne problems' is, if there is a surprise guest or not, etc. Each category has a specific amount of points assigned to it, each right guess will give you that amount of points that will add up to make 113 points total. The person with the highest amount of points will win prizes that are different each night, but the game is meant to be fun and silly, don't forget that!
* This website isn't as optimized for desktop as it is for mobile, I suggest using your mobile device in portrait mode. If you're on desktop and have recommendations, leave them to me on Twitter (@Gthlopsdki)
* I don't recommend using this website in split-screen view.

CALCULATE YOUR RESULTS
How this works is that you will enter what you guessed on Swiftball and my code will compare your guesses with the correct ones, and next to each category you will see “correct :)” or "incorrect :(”, and at the bottom of each category you will either see that you got full points or no points at all. At the end there will be a “Total” category with the sum of all your points, but you have to click “Submit” first.
* This section of the website is closed as long as Taylor is on stage, I will try to get it back as soon as possible to check your answers for the latest show. There is currently no way of checking your answers for previous shows, if you would like that, message me on Twitter (@Gthlopsdki) and I would work on it.
* Please fill every category before you click “Submit” to prevent errors.
* Please read carefully what I have to say before surprise songs as you can possibly not receive points even if my code is right.
* The actual correct answer doesn't turn green currently, check the “LATEST ANSWERS” category.
* I do not collect your data, this is for you and you only. I don't have access to it and it's not being sent to Swiftball. (I don't have either your e-mail or username)

* Don't swap your guitar and piano surprise song guesses, enter them here as you entered them in Swiftball.
* If your guitar song guess below turned out to be the piano surprise song, you will still receive 6.5 points and the message near the category would say “guess is piano acoustic” instead of “correct :)” and “incorrect :(”
* The use of acronyms is accepted here. However, it is not accepted in the actual Swiftball. Every letter of the acronym has to be capital, e.g. IOMWIWY.
* Don't include ‘(Taylor's Version)’, ‘(From The Vault)’ or features, but do include special characters (not in acronyms), eg. ‘Mr. Perfectly Fine’, ‘...ready for it?’, ‘RFI’ (acronym for ‘...ready for it?’)
* My code is case sensitive, I'll make sure it accepts guesses where the first letter of each word is capital, no letters are capital and only the first letter of the song is capital. Include apostrophes, if the song starts with ‘the’ like ‘The Other Side Of The Door’ please include it.
* Make sure that there isn't a space before or after your guess.
If your guess doesn't meet these criteria, even if it's right, it won't give you your points. Your guesses aren't hand-checked like Swiftball. Again, I don't collect your data. As soon as you click “Submit”, you will receive your points.
